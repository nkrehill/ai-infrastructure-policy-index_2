GOVERNMENT OF ALBERTA - BILL 8 & BILL 12 AMENDMENTS
ELECTRICITY UTILITIES (DATA CENTRE INTEGRATION) ACT 

REGULATORY FRAMEWORK SUMMARY:
1. Effective immediately, a strict interim connection cap of 1,200 MW is placed on all new Large Load Infrastructure projects exceeding 50 MW.
2. All data center operators requesting grid access must undergo a mandatory Phase 2 Cluster Assessment by the AESO to evaluate localized grid stability.
3. Effective December 31, 2026, a new computing equipment levy will apply to all non-power-self-sufficient operations, adding operational cost friction.
4. Projects that demonstrate "behind-the-fence" self-generation utilizing carbon-neutral or co-located off-peak assets will receive fast-tracked regulatory approvals.
